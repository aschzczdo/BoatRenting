Mallorca Island Sailing Routes

Explore the stunning beauty of Mallorca from the sea. Our curated sailing routes take you through breathtaking coastal landscapes, hidden coves, and vibrant marinas. Whether you seek serene solitude or the lively buzz of cosmopolitan ports, Mallorca offers an unmatched sailing experience.
Navigate the crystal-clear waters and discover the island in a way that only a few can.

Cala d'Or Exploration

Sail through the turquoise waters of Cala d'Or, where you can anchor and enjoy secluded beaches. Perfect for snorkeling and sunbathing, this route offers both relaxation and adventure.

Read more

Cap de Formentor Voyage

Experience the dramatic cliffs and stunning lighthouse at Cap de Formentor. This route is famous for its breathtaking views and is a must-visit for photography enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Read more

Tramuntana Coast Adventure

Explore the rugged and pristine Tramuntana Coast, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Known for its scenic landscapes and historical significance, this route offers a unique blend of natural beauty and Mallorcan heritage.

Read more

Sa Foradada Sunset Sail

Discover the iconic Sa Foradada with its distinctive rock formation and stunning sunset views. This secluded spot is accessible only by boat or a hike, making it a peaceful retreat for those looking to escape the crowds.

Read more

Deià Coastal Cruise

Explore the picturesque village of Deià, nestled in the Tramuntana Mountains along the west coast of Mallorca. This route offers stunning views of the mountainous coastline and an opportunity to visit the charming village known for its artistic heritage.

Read more

Port de Sóller Getaway

Sail into the beautiful Port de Sóller, a natural harbor surrounded by the Tramuntana mountain range. This route is perfect for those who appreciate a blend of scenic landscapes and lively local culture. Enjoy fresh seafood at the port's numerous restaurants or explore the historic tram that connects Sóller to Palma.

Read more

Cabrera Archipelago Excursion

Embark on an adventure to the Cabrera Archipelago National Park, located south of Mallorca. This protected area is home to rich marine life, pristine waters, and unspoiled islands, making it a perfect spot for diving and wildlife observation.
